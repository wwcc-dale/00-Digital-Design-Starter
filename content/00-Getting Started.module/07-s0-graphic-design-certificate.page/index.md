# Graphic Design Certificate

**Type:** One-Year Certificate
**Length:** ~60 credits
**Focus:** Design-heavy — visual design, branding, marketing, advertising
**Capstone:** BUS 289 – Graphic Design Capstone (portfolio-ready design work)

---

## What This Is

The Graphic Design Certificate goes deep on the visual side of the program.
You will master design tools, develop a strong understanding of marketing and
branding principles, and create a portfolio of professional-quality design
work to show employers.

This certificate is for students who know they want to work in design —
creating logos, brand identities, advertising campaigns, publications, and
marketing materials.

## The Capstone: BUS 289

Your capstone project will be a polished, professional-quality piece of
design work. You will choose a project type that shows your strengths.

**Examples of capstone projects:**

- A complete brand identity system: logo, color palette, typography, and
  at least three applications (business card, letterhead, social media profile)
- A multi-piece advertising campaign with consistent concept and visual voice
- A designed publication — a magazine feature, report, or booklet
- A packaging design project with multiple views and mockups

You will present your work and explain the decisions you made — why you chose
the typeface, how the color palette serves the brand, why the layout is
structured the way it is. That kind of professional reasoning is what
separates designers who get hired from those who do not.

## Who This Is For

This certificate is a strong fit if you:

- Know you want to work in design — creating visual materials, not building code
- Are interested in branding, advertising, or publication design
- Want a strong portfolio of design-focused work
- Are drawn to the relationship between design and marketing strategy
