[lead]{{var:program_name}} is a course of study that teaches you to create visual communications, build websites, and develop web applications — skills that lead to careers as a graphic designer, web developer or UX designer. You are starting something real. 

# Welcome to Digital Design

This orientation module walks you through the program, the classroom, and what's expected before you begin your first credit course.


### The Program at a Glance

Every course you complete counts toward a credential — and shorter credentials stack directly into longer ones. If your timeline changes, you leave with something real.

```
Short Certificates (starting point)
  ├── Graphic Design Essentials     ← design path starter
  └── Web Design Fundamentals       ← web path starter
· · · · · · · · · · · · · · · · · · · · · · · · · · · · · ·
One-Year Certificates (choose one path)
  ├── Digital Design Certificate    ← balanced design + web
  ├── Graphic Design Certificate    ← design-focused
  └── Web Development Certificate   ← code-focused
· · · · · · · · · · · · · · · · · · · · · · · · · · · · · ·
Two-Year AAS Degree
  └── Built on the one-year certificates
```

### Nothing is repeated. Nothing is wasted.

| [light] Credential | Credits | Length | Description |
|---|---|---|---|
| Graphic Design Essentials | 4 | 1/2 year | Design tools and principles |
| Web Design Fundamentals | 4 | 1/2 year | Interface design, HTML/CSS, JS basics |
| Digital Design Certificate | 9 | 1 year | Balanced design + web, website capstone |
| Graphic Design Certificate | 9 | 1 year | Design-heavy, design portfolio capstone |
| Web Development Certificate | 9 | 1 year | Code-heavy, web app capstone |
| AAS Degree | 18 | 2 years | Full program + general education |

After you finish this orientation, the **Degrees & Certificates** module has a dedicated page for each credential — full course lists, what each prepares you for, and the jobs it leads to.
