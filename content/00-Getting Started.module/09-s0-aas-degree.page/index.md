# Graphic Design & Web Development AAS Degree

**Type:** Associate of Applied Science Degree
**Length:** ~90 credits, approximately 2 years
**Focus:** The full program — design, marketing, web development, and general education
**Capstone:** CS 231 – Capstone Application Development

---

## What This Is

The AAS degree is the highest credential in this program. It combines
everything: graphic design, marketing, full-stack web development, and
four general education courses that build communication and critical
thinking skills.

The AAS takes the most time and the most commitment. It also opens the
most doors — to more stable employment, higher starting wages, and
stronger consideration from employers who see the degree as evidence that
you can handle complex, long-term work.

## Why the General Education Courses Matter

Some students look at the four general education courses and wonder if
they are really necessary. Here is the honest answer: they are.

**Technical Writing** — Developers and designers spend a large amount of
their work time writing. Emails, project proposals, documentation, client
communication. Professionals who write clearly stand out.

**Quantitative Problem Solving** — Web development and data management
involve math. Not calculus — practical math. Estimating time and cost,
working with numbers in code, understanding data structures. This course
builds the foundation.

**Communication in the Workplace** — Being technically skilled and not
being able to explain your work to a non-technical audience is a real
career limitation. This course directly addresses that.

The technical skills get you in the door. The communication skills keep
you there.

## Who This Is For

The AAS degree is the right choice if you:

- Have approximately two years in the program
- Want the strongest, most complete credential
- Are aiming for roles at agencies, in-house design teams, or tech companies
- Want to build a comprehensive portfolio with both design and code projects

## Jobs This Degree Prepares You For

- Graphic designer
- Front-end developer
- Full-stack developer
- UX/UI designer
- Creative technologist
- Marketing designer
- Freelance creative professional with a full range of skills

---

## The AAS and Beyond

The AAS is an applied degree focused on employment. Some students go on
to pursue a four-year degree after completing it. Check with your
instructor if that is a path you are thinking about.
